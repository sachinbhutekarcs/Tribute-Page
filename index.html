His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada

The Ambassador of Spiritual World & Founder Acharya of ISKCON (Hare Krishna Movement)

Timeline of Srila Prabhupada's Life

  • 1896 - September 01 - Calcutta - took birth
  • V1902 - At age six was performing Ratha yatra in the neighborhood
  • Śrīla Prabhupāda's mother Rajani Devi dies
  • 1916 to 1920 - Scottish Churches College run by the Scottish Church missionaries - age 20 to 24 yrs
  • 1918 - Enters married life with Radharani Datta (grhastha ashrama)
  • 1920 - Passed his final examination (B.A) at Scottish Churches College, but rejected diploma and gave up college education
  • 1922 - meets for the first time Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Gosvāmī Mahārāja—his future initiating spiritual master—and is asked to "preach this Caitanya's cult in the Western countries"
  • 1922 - Joins Gandhi's non-cooperation movement
  • 1923 - Allahabad - Leaves Calcutta to start a new business - Prayāg pharmacy
  • 1925 - Visits Vṛndāvana-dhāma for the first time and hears more from Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Mahārāja
  • 1930 - Śrīla Prabhupāda's father Gour Mohan De dies
  • 1933 - Allahabad - Accepts formal dīkṣā initiation from Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Mahārāja
  • 1935 - Visits Rādhā-kunda to see Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Mahārāja and receives instructions on constructing temples and publishing books
  • 1936 - February 12 - Vyas Puja Homage to Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Mahārāja
  • 1936 - December 31 - Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Mahārāja passes from this world leaving a message for Śrīla Prabhupāda to preach in English language
  • 944 - February 14 - Publishes the first 'Back to Godhead' magazine on Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Ṭhākura's appearance day.
  • 1947 - Receives the honorary title of 'Bhaktivedanta' from his God-brothers of the Gaudiya Math
  • 1951 - Accepts vanaprastha (retirement from family life
  • 1953 - Jhansi - Initiates first disciple - Acarya Prabhakar
  • 1953 - May 16 - Incorporates the League of devotees in Jhansi
  • 1956 - Moves to Vṛndāvana
  • 1959 - Begins translating Śrīmad Bhāgavatam & moves into an upstairs room at the Rādhā-Damodar temple
  • 1959 - September 17 - Awarded Sannyasa in Mathura by one of his Godbrothers, His Holiness B.P. Kesava Maharaja
  • 1960 - Publishes first book - 'Easy Journey to Other Planets'
  • 1964 - Publishes Volume 2, Canto 1 of Śrīmad Bhāgavatam
  • 1965
    1. Publishes Volume 3, Canto 1 of Śrīmad Bhāgavatam
    2. August 13 at 13:30 Calcutta - begins voyage to the USA aboard the Jaladuta ship
    3. September 17 at 05:30 Commonwealth Pier, Boston - historic arrival in America.
  • 1966
    1. July 13 - Incorporates the International Society for Krishna consciousness (ISKCON).
    2. September - Initiates first western disciples.
    3. October 23 - Publishes the first issue of 'Back to Godhead' magazine./li>
  • 1967 - Completed translation and commentary of Bhagavad-gītā As It Is
  • 1968 - Bhagavad-gītā As It Is abridged edition published by Macmillan
  • 1969
    1. Śrī Īśopaniṣad published by the ISKCON Press
    2. June 30 - Completed the Nectar of Devotion
    3. July 16 - Installation, Sri Sri Rukmini Dvarakanatha - Los Angeles
    4. December 14 - Rādhā-Londonisvara deity installation
  • 1970 - Establishes the ISKCON Governing Body Commission (GBC)
  • 1971 - Establishes the Mayapur-Vrindaban Trust (MVT) & Arrives in Vṛndāvana with forty western disciples
  • 1972 - Lays down the cornerstone for the Juhu Bombay Center & Establishes the Bhaktivedanta Book Trust (BBT)
  • 1973 - Śrī Śrī Rādhā Parisisvara Deity Installation
  • 1974 - Cornerstone Laying - Bombay Juhu Centre & Śrī Caitanya Caritāmṛta completed, Hare Krishna Land, Juhu, Bombay
  • 1975 - Opens Śrī Śrī Kṛṣṇa-Balarama Temple Vrindavan on Śrī Rāma-Navami
  • 1976 - Hyderabad Temple Opening
  • 1977 - Signs Declaration of Will in Vṛndāvana & Disappearances from this world at age of 81
